About the firm

Built to be your only advisor, not one of several.

Northbridge Capital Partners was founded on a simple frustration: most advisory relationships are transactional, product-driven, and short on follow-through. We work with a deliberately small number of households at a time, so every plan gets the attention it needs.

We are fee-only and fiduciary by structure — meaning we're never paid a commission to recommend a product, and we're legally bound to act in your interest first.
