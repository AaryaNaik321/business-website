About the firm

A small team, five years in, building for the long run.

N Capital Partners was founded on a simple frustration: most management relationships are transactional, product-driven, and short on follow-through. Five years on, we're still a small team by choice — six people, working closely across Indian and US markets — because it lets every plan get real attention.

We're a small business with big ambitions. The plan has always been to grow — but only in a way that doesn't cost us the thing that got us here: knowing every client's plan by name, not by account number.

We are fee-only and fiduciary by structure — meaning we're never paid a commission to recommend a product, and we're legally bound to act in your interest first.
